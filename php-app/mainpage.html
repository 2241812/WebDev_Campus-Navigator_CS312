About Us

Welcome to Bravo's Compass, your dedicated navigation companion for the campus. Designed with accessibility and ease of use in mind, our platform helps students, faculty, and visitors find the quickest paths to their destinations.

Whether you are looking for a specific classroom, the nearest elevator, or a quick shortcut, Bravo's Compass is here to guide you.

Upload Instructions

Follow these steps to upload a custom floor plan:

  1. Select Floor: Use the floor tabs at the top of the screen to switch to the specific floor you want to edit.
  2. Choose Image: Click "Choose File" in the admin panel and select your image (PNG, JPG, JPEG).
  3. Upload: Click the "Upload Floor Plan" button.
  4. Refresh: The page will reload automatically to apply the new background.
Tip: Ensure your image is high resolution for the best experience.
